Pump up the volume with the Volume Widget.

Adjust any volume type from your home screen quickly.

Features:
* Quick up/down volume adjust buttons.
* 3 different widget sizes. 1x1 2x1 4x1
* Controls all volume types. To change, tap the volume’s name.

*NOTE* This is a widget only. There’s no launcher icon. To open: Long Press Home Screen > Widgets > Volume "Size"
